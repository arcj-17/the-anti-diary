Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HumanKind Systems LLC (“Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you use the anti-diary mobile application (“App” or “Service”).

the anti-diary is designed to help users write, release, and manage private entries locally on their device.

We do not require you to create an account to use the App.

By using the App, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be stored, processed, or received when you use the anti-diary.

The App is designed around local device storage. This means your private writing content is intended to stay on your device unless a future feature clearly explains otherwise.

2. Information We Collect

We currently do not collect personal journal entries, unsent messages, email drafts, voice notes, or other writing content from the App on our own servers.

The App may store certain content locally on your device, including:

  • Written entries
  • Temporary drafts
  • Saved entries
  • Unsent message drafts
  • Unsent email drafts
  • Voice note recordings
  • App preferences
  • Purchase-related access settings

This information is stored on your device and is not sent to us unless a future feature clearly explains otherwise.

3. No Account Required

The App does not currently require you to create an account.

Because there is no account system, we do not currently collect account login information, usernames, passwords, or profile details through the App.

4. Local Storage

Your entries, drafts, unsent messages, unsent emails, and voice recordings are intended to remain local to your device.

You are responsible for managing access to your device. If someone else has access to your device, they may be able to access content stored in the App.

Depending on your device settings, Apple services such as device backup or iCloud backup may store app-related data as part of your device backup. We do not control Apple’s backup systems. You can review and manage Apple backup settings directly through your device settings.

5. Microphone Access

The App may request microphone access if you choose to use the voice note or voicemail-style feature.

Microphone access is used only to allow you to record an unsent voice note within the App.

We do not receive, store, or listen to your voice recordings on our servers.

You can disable microphone access at any time in your device settings.

6. In-App Purchases

The App may offer non-subscription in-app purchases.

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card number, banking information, or other direct payment details.

Apple may process information related to your purchase according to Apple’s own privacy policy and App Store terms.

7. Information We May Receive From You

Although we do not currently collect your private writing content, we may receive limited information if you contact us directly, such as:

  • Your email address
  • Your name, if you provide it
  • The content of your support request
  • Any information you choose to include in your message

We use this information only to respond to your request, provide support, and manage our business records.

8. Usage Data, Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

The current version of the App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking tools, or third-party crash reporting tools.

We do not use your private writing content for advertising, tracking, analytics, profiling, or marketing.

If we add analytics, ads, crash reporting, cloud storage, account features, or similar tools in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and any required App Store privacy disclosures before or when those features are released.

9. Legal Website Hosting

Our Privacy Policy, Terms and Conditions, or related website pages may be hosted by a third-party website hosting provider, such as GitHub Pages.

When you visit those web pages, the hosting provider may process basic technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and page access logs according to its own policies.

We do not use those legal pages to collect private journal content from the App.

10. How We Use Information

We may use information we receive from you for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App
  • To respond to support requests
  • To troubleshoot issues
  • To manage in-app purchase access
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the Company, and the App
  • To improve the App’s functionality and user experience

11. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share your private journal entries, unsent messages, unsent emails, or voice notes with third parties.

We may share limited information in the following situations:

  • With service providers who help us operate the App, website, payment access, or support systems

  • In connection with a business transfer, such as a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets

12. Data Retention

Content stored locally in the App remains on your device unless you delete it, remove the App, or clear the relevant content within the App.

Support emails or direct communications may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request, maintain business records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.

Because the App currently stores your private writing content locally, you can delete that content directly from the App where deletion features are available.

You may also delete the App from your device to remove locally stored app data, subject to any device backup settings managed by Apple or your device.

If you have contacted us directly and want us to delete personal information you provided through support communication, you may contact us using the email address listed below.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete that information.

If you are under the age of majority in your jurisdiction, you should use the App only with the permission of a parent or legal guardian.
